About Wayfarer

Wayfarer is the third of four projects that students were asked to complete at General Assembly's SEIR course. The goal: Create an app for a (pretend) client where users can leaves reviews about the cities they've visited! For this project, Ryan and Richard were randomly assigned together to create the masterpiece you see before you!
